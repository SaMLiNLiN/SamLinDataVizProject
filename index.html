NBA SMALL BALL ERA (Golden State Warriors)

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a professional basketball league that was founded in the United States in 1949. It consists of 82 games in the regular season, and playoff series depending on how far each team gets. There has been different eras in the history of the NBA. However, we will be focusing on the transition from the Transitional Era (1955 - 2013) to the Modern Era of the NBA (since 2013). We will be specifically focusing on the small ball playing style adopted by the Golden State Warriors (GSW), which started in approximately 2010-2011 season until around 2019-2020 season. During this period of time, it is evident that there was a change in playing time of each position, the salaries of each player, and the number of Champions each team won to show the effectiveness of the adoption of the small ball style of basketball of the GSW.

In this bar chart, we can see that in comparison to before the small ball era (2010-2011), the average playing time of both the Point guards and Small Forwards, in the team Golden State Warriors, has increased by approximately 8 mins during the beginning of the Small Ball Era (2014- 2015).

However, something to note here is that the average playing time of a shooting guard has decreased. This is due to the fact that the roster for this season contains a lot more shooting guards in the team (4 in total out of 15), therefore, when averaging the playing time it turns out to be lower as some shooting guards has less avg playing time. That being said, if we were to only look at Klay Thompson (the main SG for this team), he actually played an avg of 31.9 mins, which is the second highest (just a bit under the star player / point guard: Stephen Curry).

This shows an extreme success in the change of style for the Warriors as they focus more on players that play the point guard, shooting guard, or small forward. However, this doesn't guarantee that the small ball style will continually bring success to the Warriors because styles of basketball changes over time as players move around to different teams and new rookies enter the league. That being said, the small ball style has brought success the the Warriors in the past decade, but there will be changed to the league in the soon future, so the small ball era might come to an end sometime soon.


Summary:

After observing the three visualizations above, we can clearly see that adopting the small ball style has affected the GSW in terms of playing time for different positions, the salaries for different players and their corresponding positions, and lastly, the success of this style of basketball (focusing on PG, SG, SF) has been proven by the nubmer of championships won by the GSW in the past couple of seasons. In conclusion, the GSW is one of the most significant teams that adopted the small ball style successfully, and this has changed the league and the game of basketball as many young players starting from the youth level look up to these basketball stars and mimic their style of baseketball. That being said, the adoption of small ball style is a huge historical transition / era of basketball, and it has definintly created a huge impact in the game of basketball as well as marked a significant moment in the history of basketball!
